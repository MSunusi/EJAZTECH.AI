About EJAZTECH.AI

Driving AI innovation across Africa through education and technology

Our Overview

We specialize in effectively collecting local datasets to build innovative AI-driven solutions that automate tasks and empower individuals and organizations. Through education and knowledge sharing, we aim to make AI accessible and beneficial for everyone.

Our Mission

To create AI solutions for Africa's challenges by building local datasets, innovative models, and empowering communities through education and mentorship.

Our Vision

To be Africa's leading hub for AI innovation, driving inclusive technology that transforms industries and uplifts communities.

Our Services

Comprehensive AI solutions tailored for African markets

Data Collection & Annotation

Professional data collection and annotation services for machine learning projects, ensuring high-quality datasets for African contexts.

Model Development & Prototyping

Custom AI model development and rapid prototyping solutions designed to address specific African challenges and opportunities.

AI Consultation

Expert consultation services for AI implementation and strategy, helping organizations leverage AI for business transformation.

Mentorship Program

Charity program for the younger generation, currently running Cohort 2. We provide AI education and mentorship to build the next generation of African AI talent.
